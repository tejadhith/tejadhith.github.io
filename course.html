Course Projects

Beyond Euclidean Realms: Hyperbolic Node Embeddings

A team project under the guidance of Dr. Sumohana Channappayya as a part of the course Deep Learning [EE6380]

  • Implemented RandomWalk based embedding algorithms, Graph Neural Networks (GCNs, GraphSAGE, GATs) and Hyperbolic Graph Neural Networks (Hyperbolic GCNs - Poincare, Lorentz) in PyTorch and PyTorch Geometric.

  • Evaluated the performance of the above algorithms on various datasets (Cora, Citeseer, Pubmed) and compared the results with the state-of-the-art algorithms.

  • Achieved a test accuracy of 88.82% on Cora dataset using HGCNs-Lorentz.

Heart Rate and BP Measurement using PPG Sensor

Done under the guidance of Dr. Shubhadeep Bhattacharjee as a part of the course Electronic Devices and Circuits Lab [EE2301]

  • Measured the Heart Rate and Blood Pressure using the Photoplethysmography(PPG) signals obtained using a TCRT5000 sensor.
